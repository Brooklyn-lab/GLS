Step-by-step flow process, in a nutshell...

We use proven with years flows and partner-oriented Landing Pages, promote them through direct marketing by using online ads, and social media, and then survey motivated financial consumers.

All leads go through the initial validation process, all leads will contain relevant data.

Leads go through a credit score checker, partners choose what score level consumers they would like to get.

Buyers will get only consumers that suit them for a credit score level and according to their credentials.
